My Journey

My journey from being a normal boy to a wise poet is really interesting.
I used to wonder how writers and poets compose such great peices of art,
but it seems easy to me now. I never thought i would be able
to write poetry, but today I have more than 200 poems in my diary.

My life in breif

I was in 10th standard when I wrote my first ever motivational poem.
It was my first ever poem in English language, though I had written some
poems in Punjabi language. That poem lit a spark in me and my poetic life
began. I believe the fire which that spark lit in me still burns. I was already
in love and had a broken heart. So everything just got mixed and this gave rise
to a poet in me. Within one year from then, I wrote only 2 to 3 poems since life
became better after i left the school on completing 10th grade. I started my
higher studies at another school where I fell in love again. Amd again I got
heartbroken after being rejected. By then I had become lonely with no friends
at all. But then, I found a lost friend, poetry. I started writing just to make
myself feel better and that helped me a lot. I was in depression and used to have
mood swings, so poetry became a bestfriend of mine. Time went on and I started
writing poetry like professional poets, then came in my mind the idea to create
an instagram blog. Since then ( April 2018 ), I have been posting all my poetry
on my instagram page and it has touched hearts of thousands. This love from those
who read and appreciate my work makes me write more and more. When unknown people
praise my poetry by messaging me on instagram, I feel extremely happy. And when
people love my poems, I too feel like being loved. My poems gather love for me,
which I myself could never do .

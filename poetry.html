              My Poetry in English

English is a worldwide spoken language and this made me write most of my poems in English
language, so that, my work can be read by a more wide audience. I have more than 200 poems
written in english, though I haven't posted all of them, neither on my blog ( this website )
nor on my instagram page. Some of the poems on this website have a handwritten background while
some I have created with photo editing applications.


Important : The photos in the background of the poems have been taken from Pexels.
 And the one line drawings in some photos were taken from Pinterest.




Inked on Paper

Technology has had an impact on everything in this world and poets are no exception to that.
There was a time when poetry and prose used to be written by hand on a paper, but these days,
many writers use the notes application on their phones to write. But still there are some
writers who prefer inking the words instead of typing them. I am too one of those passionate
writers. Though I also use the notes application when I create a post for my instagram page
or my website ( this blog ).

Inspiration

Inspiration is very important if we want to achieve something in life.
One can get inspired from a successful person or sometimes even self.

                What was my inspiration ?

I was in 10th standard when I got inspired to write poetry
and I got inspired by 3 writers whose names I will discuss below.







Writers that inspired me

Henry Wadsworth Longfellow

The peom 'A psalm of life' written by Henry inspired me to such an extent that
I spent hours understanding the depth of the poem. This impacted my writing style
too as I also write the poems with a deep meaning

William Shakespeare

William Shakespeare is my favourite writer and I have been inspired a lot from his
writing style.

Nissim Ezekiel

'The Professor' poem by Nissim Ezekiel inspired me a lot. This poem is a free style
poem and does not follow any rhyming sequence or patter. I too do the same type of poetry.

My suggestion to new writers

Writers start their writing career but they lack knowledge regarding how to
reach a greater audience. Therefore, i want to suggest young writers to
try the following social media sites to grow their reach.

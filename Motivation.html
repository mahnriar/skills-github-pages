Motivation

Motivation is all that we need sometimes in life.
Motivation does not solve our problems, but it gives us the courage to do so. Even a normal
person can do miracles if motivated, such is the power of motivation. In addition to poetry,
I also post motivational quotes on my instagram page.


Important : The photos in the background of the poems/quotes have been taken from Pexels.
 And the one line drawings in some photos were taken from Pinterest.




Me and Motivation

We humans have become habitual of being motivated by others, due to which we try little to
motivate ourselves. But not always do we have somebody around us to motivate us. In such
a situation, we struggle with ourselves about the lack of motivation in life. I too feel demotivated
sometimes due to which I become sad and then I try motivating myself by writing something, especially
motivational quotes and poems And sometimes I watch my favourite motivational video on youtube.
The quotes I write serve a dual purpose for me, I not only motivate myself but also the people who read
my work on my blog. This brings
me happiness.
